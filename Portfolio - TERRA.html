TERRA VIVA COMPETITIONS

Project by Luis Felipe Nunes ; Daniela Cordeiro
Category Visual Identity Design ; Contest
Year 2023

What the h*ll
is this thing?
Why this project?

We found an graphic design competition somewhere on the internet. A small italian company that wanted to change their logo. After understanding more about the company and reading through the brief, we ended up deciding to participate in the competition. We didn't win (which is sad) but we ended up being featured on their comunication channels.





What Was the Plan?


Since the brand wanted to shift their appearance completly, we decided to go in a very different way.
(Side note: The chosen was the proposal that looked as similar as their older one)
We also decided to keep their colour pallete since the logo change would change a lot, at least the colours would ease
the shift from the past to the new era of the brand.
