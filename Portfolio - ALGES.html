ALGES, VISUALY SPEAKING

Project by Luis Felipe Nunes
Category Visual Identity Design ; Academic
Year 2023




Do you even
know Alges?


It was proposed for us to develop a visual identity based on the physical environment of a municipality around Lisbon.
I choose alges, not a very big town that works almost as a meeting spot coming from any part of Lisbon. It is possible to go to alges from any location of Lisbon.
And that's what makes alges such a special place, it crosses people's paths.
