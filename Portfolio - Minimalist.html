MINIMALIST TO-DO APP

Project by Luis Felipe Nunes
Category UX | UI Design ; Acadamical
Year 2022


The Story behind it...

In the last semester I had a subject called interaction design that
basically existed for us to know a little more about UI and UX. The first exercise that was given to us was to create an app based on a classmate.
I ended up working with a colleague named Isabel and during our little conversation she mentioned that she was very disorganised and that she had problems keeping track of what she had to do. I decided to create a minimalist to-do list app for her.

What this experience brought

With this project I started understanding how difficult it is to put ourselves in other people's shoes. This experience was not graded so I never got feedback on how to better solve a problem like this one. I found this type of project to be very exciting and challanging.

I always enjoy a good challange!
