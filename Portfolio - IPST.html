FUEL FOR DREAMS - IPST+HSJ

Project by Luis Felipe Nunes, Daniela Cordeiro e Maria
Category Visual Identity Design ; Advertising ; Campaigns
Year 2024

Why did we turn
blood into fuel for dreams?



Most humans are dream driven. That's a fact that we cannot deny but there is people out there that cannot dream anymore. This project was created for them. For those that don't even the strengh to keep fighting for what they want; for those who urgently need blood donations.

This project was brought to us by Saint John's Hospital in Lisbon and the Portuguese Institute of blood and transplantation. They've noticed that the younger public was not donating that much and, since Portugal has a very old population, they really need the new generations to become regular donnors as the older ones did.


As long as there is blood to be pumped there will be strengh to follow your dreams and those who do not have blood
do not have the fuel to chase their dreams.




But how does this make young people
donate more often?


Sometimes simple questions can have difficult answers but this is not the case.

Young people love to dream, we all dreamed of having our own car or a nice house and, with the right speech and a little bit of
emotional mechanisms, we can guide their thoughts to the point where they realise that 30 minutes of their time could save someone's
dream. The target wouldn't want for their dreams to be stopped by some liquid and end up feeling empathy and becoming donnors.






Was the proposal accepted?


Well, not yet. But we very high expectations. We really think that we can make a great impact if our project gets chosen by the jury.
Winning or not, developing a project for such a great cause to a institution as big as the IPST (Portuguese Institude of the Blood and Transplantation)
is always intering and challanging. We believe this may be one of our biggest projects.
