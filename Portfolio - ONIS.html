BOLSA SOCIAL ONIS

Project by Luis Felipe Nunes, Daniela Cordeiro e Maria
Category Visual Identity Design
Year 2023

A little context
about the project

This project got to our hand in a university environment. One of the courses had a project assignment that normally is a problem brought by a real institution and all students would develop solutions that if appropriate could be chosen to be applied. We were challenged to Design the Visual Identity for "Bolsa Social ONIS" that is ONIS' social fund. ONIS is a Portuguese non-governmental institution that helps families in need to get medical diagnosis for neurotypical children.


Among other 150+ other students, our project was the chosen one to continue the development and to be used.
